# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and inclusive environment.
This project exists to demonstrate automation, CI/CD, and DevOps best practices,
and all participants are expected to contribute professionally.

Harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Providing thoughtful and actionable feedback
- Accepting criticism gracefully
- Keeping communication professional and on-topic

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Discrimination of any kind
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any behavior that undermines a collaborative environment

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions related to this repository,
including issues, pull requests, discussions, and external communication
related to the project.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit,
or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in being banned from contributing.

---

## Contact

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it
by opening a private issue or contacting the repository owner directly.
